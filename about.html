About Atelier Anna

A Natural Wordsmith at Heart

Less than a decade ago, Anna was at the height of her creative career, but she felt completely burnt out and had little to no attachment to the corporate work. So Anna began carrying a sketchbook around with me wherever she went, whether it was chilling at a coffee shop or traveling the world. She started putting pictures with words whenever and wherever she could, and these notebooks began filling with up with joy, humor, and beautiful ideas.

The practice of doing a little bit of writing each day nurtured her soul and has greatly improved her well-being, so she decided to create Atelier Anna, a creative studio where she shares out daily prose via her email newsletter.

Anna has helped dozens of folks learn the art of creative juxtoposition with visuals and words through her workshops and daily writing. Bring a little bit of creativity into your inbox everyday by signing up below to receive daily prose.
